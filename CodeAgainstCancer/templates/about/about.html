About CodeAgainstCancer

CodeAgainstCancer is a non-profit organization that aims to help cancer patients and their families by providing them with the resources they need to fight cancer. We understand that cancer can be a difficult journey. We are here to help you every step of the way. Below are some resources that can help you get the support you need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help cancer patients and their families by providing them with the resources they need to fight cancer.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where cancer is no longer a life-threatening disease.
